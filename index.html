CHILDREN’S HEALTH PROGRAM

There are a billion people living without clean water. In September 2007, we had a crazy idea. We asked our friends to give up their birthdays and ask for donations instead of gifts. We used 100% of the money raised to build water projects. Every year, it got bigger. We’ve now helped over a million people in 17 countries get clean water.

Watch the Trailer.

Visit charitywater.org/september.

Start a campaign and give up your birthday.

It can change the world.
